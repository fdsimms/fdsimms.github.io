About Me

Like the header says, I'm equal parts web developer, musician and linguaphile. To me, they each represent a great deal of what's important: creativity, meaningful communication and a drive toward self-improvement. Coding is awesome on its own, but it also acts as an intersection between my other interests. Although I could write a folk song about JavaScript (and, let's face it, probably will), it's much more rewarding to build out a music app from the ground up.

I recently completed App Academy—a three-month, 1000-hour full-stack web development course in downtown NYC. While there, I cut my teeth on tools like React.js, PostgreSQL, and Ruby on Rails. Now I'm looking to join a team of developers working on interesting problems. So whether you're currently on the hunt for engineers or you're just here to visit, feel free to click around a while, and be sure to check out my contact info below if you want to reach out.
